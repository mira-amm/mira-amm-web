## Our Vision

The goal of MIRA is to create a dynamic and customizable liquidity landscape.

By enabling permissionless incentive mechanisms, protocols can boost their liquidity through bribes and other incentives, resulting in deeper markets and a better trading environment for all users.

## Roadmap

1. **Phase 1 - Basic AMM (Current):** Deploying as a basic Automated Market Maker to establish the foundational platform.

2. **Phase 2 - Transition to ve(3,3):** Evolving into a ve(3,3) model to introduce advanced governance and incentive mechanisms for long-term sustainability.
